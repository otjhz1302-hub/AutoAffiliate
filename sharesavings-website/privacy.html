ShareSavings.io

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to ShareSavings.io ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services.

By using ShareSavings.io, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We may collect the following personal information:

  • Name and email address (when you subscribe or register)
  • Account credentials (username and encrypted password)
  • Payment information (processed securely through third-party providers)
  • Communication preferences
  • Feedback and correspondence

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • IP address and geographic location
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information (type, operating system)
  • Pages visited and time spent on site
  • Referring website addresses
  • Clicks and interactions with coupons and affiliate links

2.3 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Track affiliate link clicks and conversions
  • Analyze site usage and performance (Google Analytics)
  • Serve personalized content and advertisements
  • Enable social media features (Facebook Pixel, Meta Graph API)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: To provide, maintain, and improve our coupon and affiliate services
  • Personalization: To customize content, deals, and recommendations based on your interests
  • Communication: To send newsletters, deal alerts, and promotional offers (with your consent)
  • Affiliate Tracking: To track clicks, conversions, and calculate cashback rewards
  • Analytics: To understand usage patterns and improve user experience
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security threats
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes

4. Third-Party Sharing and Disclosure

We share your information with third parties only as described below:

4.1 Affiliate Partners

When you click on affiliate links, certain information (click data, device info) is shared with:

  • Amazon Associates
  • eBay Partner Network
  • AliExpress Affiliate Program
  • TikTok Shop Affiliate Program
  • Shein Affiliate Program
  • Walmart Affiliate Program

4.2 Analytics and Advertising

  • Google Analytics: Website usage analysis and reporting
  • Facebook Pixel/Meta: Retargeting and conversion tracking for social media ads
  • Advertising Networks: To display relevant ads based on your interests

4.3 Service Providers

We work with third-party service providers for:

  • Website hosting and infrastructure
  • Email delivery services
  • Payment processing (if applicable)
  • Customer support tools

4.4 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or governmental request, or to:

  • Comply with legal processes
  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Protect rights, property, or safety
  • Investigate fraud or security issues

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services and fulfill transactions
  • Comply with legal obligations (tax, accounting records)
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements
  • Improve our services and user experience

After the retention period, we securely delete or anonymize your data. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails or disable cookies
  • Object: Object to processing of your data for certain purposes
  • Restrict: Request limitation of data processing

To exercise these rights, please contact us at: privacy@sharesavings.io

7. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
  • Secure database storage with encryption at rest
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Access controls and authentication protocols
  • Employee training on data protection

However, no system is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have data protection laws different from your jurisdiction.

When we transfer data internationally, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, such as:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (EU)
  • Privacy Shield Framework (where applicable)
  • Adequacy decisions by relevant authorities

9. Children's Privacy

ShareSavings.io is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal data, we will delete it immediately.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with information, please contact us at: privacy@sharesavings.io

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on this page with a new "Last Updated" date
  • Sending an email notification (if you've subscribed)
  • Displaying a prominent notice on our website

Your continued use of our services after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
