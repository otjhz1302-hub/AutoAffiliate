Frequently Asked Questions

How does ShareSavings.io work?

ShareSavings.io aggregates the best coupons and deals from major retailers. When you click our links and make purchases, we earn affiliate commissions and share a portion with you through our cashback program.

Is it really free?

Yes! ShareSavings.io is completely free to use. We earn commissions from retailers, not from you.

How do I get cashback?

Simply click on our affiliate links before making a purchase. Your cashback will be tracked automatically and credited to your account once the purchase is confirmed by the retailer.

Which retailers do you support?

We partner with Amazon, eBay, Shein, AliExpress, TikTok Shop, Walmart, and many more platforms.

How is my privacy protected?

We take privacy seriously and comply with GDPR and CCPA. We only collect necessary data and never sell your personal information. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Can I become an affiliate partner?

Yes! Visit our Affiliate Program page to learn how to join and start earning.
