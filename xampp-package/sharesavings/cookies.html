Cookie Policy

Last updated: November 2025

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit ShareSavings.io. They help us provide better services, track affiliate links, and improve your browsing experience.

Types of Cookies We Use

1. Essential Cookies

Required for basic website functionality (login sessions, preferences).

2. Analytics Cookies

Track site usage via Google Analytics to improve performance.

3. Affiliate Tracking Cookies

Track clicks on Amazon, eBay, TikTok Shop, and other affiliate links for commission purposes.

4. Advertising Cookies

Used by Meta Pixel and other platforms for retargeting and ads.

How to Manage Cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

Third-Party Cookies

We use services from Google Analytics, Meta/Facebook, Amazon Associates, and other partners that may place their own cookies.
