How to Request Data Deletion

If you wish to permanently delete all your data and account from ShareSavings.io, follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to: delete@sharesavings.io
  2. Subject line: "Data Removal Request"
  3. Include:
    • Your full name
    • Email address associated with your account
    • Any additional identifiers (username, if applicable)

What Will Be Deleted

  • Your account information (name, email, preferences)
  • All saved coupons and favorites
  • Communication history
  • Analytics and usage data tied to your account
  • Any other personal information we store

What Cannot Be Deleted

For legal and compliance reasons, we may retain:

  • Transaction records (tax, accounting compliance)
  • Aggregated, anonymized analytics data
  • Records required by law or court order
