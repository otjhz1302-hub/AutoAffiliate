Meta/Facebook Compliance

Meta Platform Usage

ShareSavings.io uses Meta-approved integrations including Facebook Login, Instagram Graph API, Meta Pixel, and Facebook Business Tools.

Data Handling

  • We do not store, resell, or misuse Facebook user data
  • All integrations comply with Meta Developer Terms and Platform Policy
  • User data is processed securely and only for authorized purposes
  • We implement required data deletion callbacks

Data Deletion Instructions

To delete your Facebook data from ShareSavings.io:

  1. Go to Facebook Settings → Apps and Websites
  2. Find "ShareSavings.io" and click Remove
  3. Or email privacy@sharesavings.io
